#  Day 6 – Cloud Service Models & Shared Responsibility Model

##  Topics Covered

* Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)
* Platform as a Service (PaaS)
* Software as a Service (SaaS)
* Comparison of IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS

##  What I Learned

Today, I explored the three major cloud service models and understood how they differ in terms of management, flexibility, and use cases. I also learned about the Shared Responsibility Model, which explains how security responsibilities are divided between the cloud provider and the customer.

Additionally, I gained an overview of the three leading cloud providers—AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform—and their popular services.

## Key Takeaways

* Understood the differences between IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS.
* Learned who manages what in each service model.
* Explored the Shared Responsibility Model.
* Studied the benefits of cloud services.
* Learned about the major cloud platforms and their common services.
